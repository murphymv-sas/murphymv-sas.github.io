About Michael Murphy

Thank you for visiting! Michael Murphy is a Master of Behavioral and Decision Sciences (MBDS) candidate at the University of Pennsylvania. He earned his B.S. in Neuroscience with a Health Studies Certificate from Dickinson College in Carlisle, PA in May of 2020. His professional interests include health, design thinking, and sustainability.

After graduating from the MBDS program in August 2021, Michael plans to work as a consultant and/or with health tech startups. In his free time he enjoys running with his dog Oscar, attending CrossFit classes, and listening to podcasts.
